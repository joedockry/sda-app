     About

Established in 1999, SDA has developed an expansive portfolio of projects including, Public Houses, Hotels and Leisure Centres, Offices and Commercial Buildings, Nursing Homes and Medical Centres and both single and multi unit Dwellings, in low density and high-density urban environments. SDA also maintains a high level of investment in modern technology and professional development training of personnel.

Along with our wide range of architectural services, SDA offers complementary and creative Interior Design solutions for projects in both the commercial and private sector.

These services include initial design concept, detailed tender packages, working and detailed drawings, specifying and sourcing of all fixtures and fittings, on site contract administration and project management for jobs ranging from Bar, Restaurant and Hotel Design (including Leisure centres) to refurbishment of existing premises and the design and fit-out of private houses, show-houses, apartments and offices.
